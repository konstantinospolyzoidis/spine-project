Welcome to The Spine Project

Our mission is to educate both medical students and the public about the spine's anatomy, function, common disorders, and strategies for maintaining spinal health.

Did you know? The human spine consists of 33 vertebrae. 7 cervical, 12 thoracic, 5 lumbar, 5 fused sacral, and 4 fused coccygeal
