Welcome to The Spine Project

Our mission is to educate medical students and the general public about the spine, its importance, its common conditions, and ways to keep the human spine healthy.

Did you know? The human spine consists of 33 vertebrae. 7 cervical, 12 thoracic, 5 lumbar, 5 sacral (fused), and 4 coccygeal (fused)

Explore the site to learn more about spinal anatomy, physiology, pathology, and health tips. Let's build this together.
